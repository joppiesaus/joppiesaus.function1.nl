Searching tips

  • Having a property: has:variable Example: has:url
  • Equals: has:variable(equals) or variable:equals Example: title:foo
  • Contains: contains:variable(equals) or just no code. Example: contains:title(Sheep) or with arrays: variable:has(property). Example: tags:has(Web)
  • Invert stuff with !. Example: !tags:has(Game) will select everything that's not a game.
